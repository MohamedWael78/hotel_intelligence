DATABASE ENGINEERING

Relational Schema Design

11 normalized tables across 4 functional domains — Reservations as the central hub, normalized to 3NF throughout.

Design Principle
The Reservations entity acts as a hub with 4 FKs linking Hotels, Guests, Rooms, and Staff. A 1:1 Reservation→Invoice relationship and ServiceUsage junction table deliberately separate operational and billing analytics. All 119,390 rows ingested via a 4-stage ETL pipeline in under 30 seconds.
Master Data6 Tables
  • Hotels — HotelID, Name, City, StarRating
  • RoomTypes — TypeID, Name, Capacity, BasePrice
  • Rooms — UNIQUE(HotelID, RoomNum)
  • Guests — GuestID, Email, IsRepeatedGuest
  • Staff — Role, Department, HotelID FK
  • Services — Name, Category, Price
Transactional4 Tables
  • Reservations — 4×FK hub, Status, Channel, Nights
  • ServiceUsage — M:N junction (Reservation×Service)
  • Invoices — 1:1 with Reservation, OutstandingAmount
  • Payments — 1:N under Invoice, partial payments OK
Ops + Staging1+1 Tables
  • HousekeepingLogs — StartTime, EndTime, CleaningType
  • HotelBooking_Staging — flat NVARCHAR buffer for CSV
  • 4-stage ETL: Load → Validate → Normalize → Error-log
  • 119,390 rows ingested in under 30 seconds

BUSINESS RECOMMENDATIONS

6 Actionable Strategies

Derived from EDA findings and validated by ML. Structured by implementation priority.

🔴 Immediate Priority
Deploy Real-Time Cancellation Risk Score
Integrate into PMS: lead_time>120d (+2pts) + Non-Refund deposit (+3pts) + prior_cancellations≥1 (+2pts). Score≥5 triggers re-confirmation workflow 14 days pre-arrival. AUC 0.962 validates signal accuracy.
Impact: Manage ~15% of high-risk inventory proactively
🔴 Immediate Priority
Revise Non-Refundable OTA Deposit Policy
The ~99% cancellation rate for Non-Refund OTA bookings is a systemic policy failure. Negotiate true pre-auth captures with OTA partners or discontinue Non-Refund rate plans on OTA channels entirely.
Impact: Eliminate the deposit-cancellation paradox
🟡 Medium-Term
Launch Direct Booking Incentive Programme
Direct bookings deliver higher ADR and lower cancellation than any OTA channel. Offer guaranteed upgrade + flexible cancellation. A 5% OTA→Direct shift saves ~2.3pp cancellation rate.
Impact: −2.3pp cancel rate · higher ADR
🟡 Medium-Term
Special Request Solicitation at Booking
Each additional special request reduces cancellation probability by ~8%. Automated post-booking email asking for preferences deepens guest commitment at zero marginal cost.
Impact: Reduce cancellation via psychological investment
🔵 Strategic
Structured Loyalty Programme
Repeat guests cancel at 15% vs 40% — a 25pp differential. Converting 1 new→repeat guest eliminates 2.7× the cancellation risk. Tiered benefits drive conversion.
Impact: Each repeat conversion = 2.7× risk reduction
🔵 Strategic
Power BI Revenue Management Dashboard
Deploy Power BI via DirectQuery to SQL Server using the 10 analytical views as the data layer. Live occupancy, ADR by room type, outstanding receivables, and staff performance — all unified.
Impact: Real-time operational decision support
